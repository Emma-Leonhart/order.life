Its central deity is alo, the emanating cosmos

Its goal is agency which is a concept related to being able to impose an order on the world. This is a collectivistic goal.

At its core it revolves around delivering the Three Fruits to everyone, or colloquially getting everyone to Omega.

Agency is delivered to people through three methods: freeing them from death, freeing them from the nine plagues, and freeing them from Cosmic destruction.

Universalism is a critical element of Lifeism. The idea of anyone being excluded goes extremely counter to its values, As such Lifeism identifies itself in contrast with traditions which are exclusionary, especially on traits like race, sexual orientation, gender identity, or social class.

As an extention of universalism Lifeism engages in syncretism with other traditions. However it is not a syncretic tradition in itself, and is fully capable of standing on its own

The only Evolutionist organization currently in existence is the Church of Evolution run by Immanuelle

The tradition is referred to by multiple names across languages. Each name emphasizes different nuances such as linguistic register, cultural resonance, scope, or formality. None of the names is intended as a complete doctrinal statement; rather, they function as labels suited to different contexts.

Lifeism

[edit | edit source]

Lifeism is a plain English descriptive term centered on life as the primary referent. It has a modern, minimalist tone and is used mainly for accessibility in English-language contexts.

The term does not imply a specific metaphysical position or hierarchy of life; rather, it functions as a broad label emphasizing continuity, vitality, and living processes. Because of its abstract and secular sound, it is often used where technical clarity or neutrality is desired.

Beliefs

[edit | edit source]

Lifeism values agency and growth highly. It believes in a cosmic principle of alo, sometimes called God, or God-Alo.

Lifeism sees life as being a puzzle, not a struggle, and that there is always an easy way out of a problem which is often ignored. As such Lifeism strives to avoid war and violence in favor of solving problems the easy way.

Lifeism believes that physical mortality is a substantial obstacle towards self actualization, and as such should be abolished, and that intelligence naturally subverts itself due to the nine plagues. Escaping this subverion must be done through puzzle solving, it cannot be done through brute force.

Lifeism focuses substantially on the Fermi paradox which is seen as the ultimate puzzle which will determine if life can survive.

Practices

[edit | edit source]

Lifeism follows a system of practices built around science and philosophy

Initiatory ethics

[edit | edit source]

Ethics is seen as initiatory with gradual increases in obligations rather than something static. Initiaiton is generally seen as important with little expectations of non-initiatory practice

Marriage levels

[edit | edit source]
Main article: Syzygy

Marriages are at three levels: omega lambda and alpha corresponding to someone's level of initiation. Coronation being Celestial initiation or Patriarchal Priesthood initiation allows for celestial marriage

The basic notion behind this is to prevent divorce by enforcing a vetting process to ensure people have the will and resources to stay married. This ensures children can be more reliably born with good lives, and as such the population will increase

Covenants

[edit | edit source]

There are three covenants. Someone must join one and then wait a certain period and meet certain criteria to join the next. They are seen as cosmologically important and reflective of the nature of the universe

  1. Omega covenant
  2. Lambda covenant
  3. Alpha covenant

Coronation

[edit | edit source]
Main article: Coronation

Coronation is moving to the Alpha Covenant. It is a massive system in itself

Sangha democracy

[edit | edit source]
Main article: Sangha Democracy

Sangha democracy is the notion of prides locally running themselves and determining their laws. Such will be in a system based on Athenian democracy. It has three main goals.

  • Legitimization of firmlaw for individuals
  • Tailoring firmlaw to local areas
  • Raising up of the individual themselves by placing them in such debates.
