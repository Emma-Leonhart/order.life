The The Realm of Hokkaido is a realm in Japan.

It's name means "North Sea Way" 

It used to be called Ezo.

It was originally inhabited by the Ainu 

As a victim of settler colonialism Hokkaido has many lost secrets.

Old provinces: Oshima, Shiribeshi, Iburi, Ishikari, Teshio, Kitami, Hidaka, Tokachi, Kushiro, Nemuro.
