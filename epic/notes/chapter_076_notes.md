# Chapter 76: "The Ocean's Death" - Notes

## Timeline & Setting
- **Period**: Latest Permian (~252.2 MYA)
- **Environmental Context**: Marine ecosystem collapse
- **Geological Context**: Ocean chemistry crisis

## Key Characters
- **Anoxia** (without oxygen) - Ocean suffocation spirit
- **Hydrogen Sulfide** - Toxic gas killer
- **Reef Death** - Collapse of marine ecosystems
- **Purple Waters** - Hydrogen sulfide ocean color

## Major Themes
- **Ocean Anoxia**: Marine oxygen depletion
- **Toxic Oceans**: Hydrogen sulfide poisoning marine life
- **Reef Collapse**: Complex marine ecosystems dying
- **Marine Mass Death**: Ocean life experiencing massive mortality

## Scientific Elements
- **Ocean Acidification**: pH dropping from volcanic CO2
- **Anoxic Conditions**: Oxygen levels plummeting in oceans
- **Hydrogen Sulfide Production**: Bacterial production of toxic gas
- **Marine Extinction**: 96% of marine species dying

## Environmental Context
- Ocean chemistry completely disrupted
- Marine food webs collapsing entirely
- Toxic conditions making oceans uninhabitable
- Atmosphere becoming poisonous from ocean gases

## Narrative Arc
- Ocean ecosystems suffocating and poisoning
- Marine life experiencing unprecedented mortality
- Complex reef systems collapsing completely
- Oceans becoming toxic wasteland
