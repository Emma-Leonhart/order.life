# Chapter 021 Notes: The Eukaryotic Wedding
*Aquarius 21 - Chapter 21 of 364*

## Chapter Content Summary
This chapter narrates the foundational marriage of Nectarius and Ambrosia, representing the emergence of complex eukaryotic cells after the end of global glaciation. It chronicles their four children who establish the major eukaryotic kingdoms: Animus (animals), Plantus (plants), Opima (mobile protists), and Cortica (fungi/storage specialists). The narrative focuses particularly on the endosymbiotic events that created chloroplasts and mitochondria.

## The Great Marriage
### Nectarius and Ambrosia's Union
- **Post-Glacial Setting**: "In the warming seas where ice had ruled" - Ediacaran Period emergence
- **Divine Blessing**: "Great Mithra blessed their union from above" - cosmic approval
- **Eukaryotic Foundation**: "Would bear the heirs of eukaryotic might"
- **Dual Wisdom**: Nectarius's power + Ambrosia's wisdom from Jingu lineage

### Environmental Context
- **Warming World**: "The frozen world no longer was fooled" - end of Snowball Earth
- **Creative Power**: "Life burst forth with new creative power" - Cambrian explosion prelude
- **Golden Light**: "Warming oceans danced with golden light" - increased solar energy
