## Archetype of Recovery
- **Most Optimistic**: Because mammals (synapsids) finally reclaim dominance
- **Synapsid Return**: Completes the synapsid → sauropsid → synapsid cycle
- **Full Circle**: Returns to synapsid rule after 186 million years of "exile"
- **Resurrection Theme**: Ultimate Easter - death leading to new life

## K-Pg Extinction Mechanics
- **Chicxulub Impact**: Six-mile asteroid impact winter
- **Deccan Volcanism**: Massive flood basalt volcanism
- **Global Catastrophe**: Nuclear winter conditions, acid rain, food web collapse
- **Clean Kill Switch**: Comprehensive enough to eliminate technological civilization
- **Non-Avian Dinosaur Extinction**: Ends the age of giants

## Dual Survivor Strategy
- **Birds**: Dinosaur heritage continues through avian line
  - "Pigeons of the sauropod dream"
  - Flying capability enables survival
  - Represent dinosaur civilization's continuation
- **Mammals**: Synapsid heritage finally resurges
  - "Children of the mouse of the synapsid kings"
  - Small size and adaptability enable survival
  - Represent return of synapsid dominance

## Calendar Completion
- **Pisces 28**: Final Easter-capable date in Gaian calendar
- **Cycle Completion**: Ends 29-chapter Easter cycle
- **Optimistic Resolution**: Unlike other extinctions, leads to human emergence
- **Resurrection Fulfillment**: True Easter - death enabling higher consciousness
