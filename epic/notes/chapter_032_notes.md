## Chapter Content Summary
This chapter focuses on Ectus, the fierce predator among the three patriarchs, detailing his evolutionary innovations and the diversification of his descendants into various worm lineages. It traces the evolution of predation and the development of ecdysozoan animals through biblical genealogical metaphors.
