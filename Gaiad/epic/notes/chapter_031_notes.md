# Chapter 031 Notes: Rodinia Supercontinent Forms
*Capricorn 5 - Chapter 31 of 364*

## Chapter Content Summary
This chapter narrates the beginning of the Cambrian Explosion through the story of two brothers, Protos and Dutrus, who inherit different interpretations of their parents' body plan. Their philosophical and biological differences lead to warfare, driving rapid evolutionary innovation and the diversification of animal life forms.

## The Great Schism
### Inheritance Crisis
- **Cambrius and Spriggina**: "Great heroes who had ruled with pride" - deceased parents
- **Two Sons**: Protos and Dutrus, each inheriting different aspects
- **Legacy Interpretation**: "Each brother saw a different way to honor what their parents' legacy would say"

### Protos's Conservative Path (Protostomes)
- **Mouth First**: "The first hole is the mouth so wide, the second is where waste will hide"
- **Traditional Development**: "I'll keep the ancient norm"
- **Mesoderm as Mesoglea**: "The mesoderm is like the past, mesoglea that will last"
- **Tentacled Mouth**: "My mouth shall bear tentacles bright like fathers did in ancient sight"
- **Three Germ Layers**: "Endoderm, ectoderm, three—the mesoderm—shall ever be!"

### Dutrus's Progressive Path (Deuterostomes)
- **Mouth Second**: "The second hole's the mouth, I say, the first hole takes waste away"
- **Innovation**: "The mesoderm is something more than what we've seen before"
- **Cellular Reunion**: "Choanoderm and Archaeocyte together in the fight"
- **Progressive Philosophy**: "By joining old with new again we'll build a glorious reign"
