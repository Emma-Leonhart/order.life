## Biological/Geological Connections
- **Late Devonian extinction** (Frasnian-Famennian boundary)
- **Kellwasser and Hangenberg events** - real extinction pulses
- **Reef collapse** - massive loss of Devonian reef ecosystems
- **Trilobite decline** - major group nearly extinct
- **Eurypterid extinction** - sea scorpions vanished
- **Graptolite extinction** - colonial marine animals extinct
- **Placoderm extinction** - armored fish disappeared
- **Ammonoid survival** - cephalopods continued but reduced
- **100 million year reef gap** - real geological phenomenon

### Environmental Factors
- **Ocean anoxia** (Kellwasser events)
- **Climate change** and cooling
- **Volcanism** and atmospheric changes
- **Marine vs. terrestrial** differential survival

The chapter serves as both an account of the Late Devonian mass extinction and a spiritual meditation on the importance of heeding prophetic warnings, learning from history, and maintaining faith through catastrophic change. It emphasizes community solidarity and collective memory as keys to surviving recurring cycles of destruction.
