# Chapter 49 Notes

## The Great Terrestrial Colonization and the First Partnerships

### The Marine Exodus
- **Sons of Cambrius** flourished in seas
- **Symbio-Mygus** (fungi) and **Charina-Plantus** (plants) pushed frontiers
- Faced competition, needed new territories

### Euthy's Desperate Leap
- **Euthy** (daughter of Mandibulus) chased by **Anomalocaris**
- Protecting precious eggs, **jumped through shimmering sky to land**
- **First arthropod to make the sea-to-land leap**
- **Suffocated in thin air** but protected her legacy
- Both she and husband died but **eggs survived**

## Myry the Trepanated: Breathing Innovation

### The Piercing Solution
- **Myry** - one of Euthy's daughters, **suffocated in water** 
- Father **pierced holes above her ten thousand legs**
- Through holes she received **Hausa's blessings** (air) directly
- **First arthropod breathing system** for terrestrial life
- Her descendants = **Myriapods** (millipedes and centipedes)

### Myry's Coast Discovery
- **Explored new lands**, found **lichen cities**
- Originally called **"Myry's coast"** but renamed **"Coast of Three Kingdoms"**
- Discovered cities **"born of covenant between Symbio-Mygus and Charina-Planta"**

## The Lichen Cities: First Terrestrial Partnership

### Symbiotic Architecture
- **Sprawling cities** half mold (fungi) and moss (plants)
- **Mycelium delved deep** for earthly nutrients
- **Thallus basked in sun** for photosynthesis
- **When cities met, they married** - fungi and plants wedding
- **"Each nation wed the other like sister and like brother"**

### Myry's Impact
- **Ate the cities whole** - destructive but unintentionally beneficial
- **Scattered cells everywhere** as she ran around
- **Messy eating** left pieces for others to use
- Enabled **global dispersal** of symbiotic partnerships

## Cain and Abel: The Great Myriapod Divergence

### The Offering
- **Myry's sons**: **Cain** and **Abel** (gentle, mild)
- **Cain brought fruit** of the ground
- **Abel brought best of flocks**
- **Mithra accepted Abel's offering**, rejected Cain's
- Cain became **wroth**, **murdered Abel** in the field

### The Curse and Consequences
- **Mithra's curse**: ground won't give strength to Cain
- Cain became **wanderer**, marked with **sevenfold vengeance** protection
- Went to **land of Nod**, built city named after son **Centipus**
- **Cain → ancestor of Caintipedes** (centipedes) - **hunters with poison bite**
- **Abel's wife → ancestor of millipedes** - **gentle decomposers**

### Abel's Legacy
- Abel's body fell **"in million pieces small"**
- His pregnant wife became **ancestress of millipedes**
- **Millipedes are mild**, "harm not beast or child"
- **Eat fallen leaves** and **decompose them all**

## The Fungal Love Story: Hera and Freyr

### Death and Resurrection
- **Myry and Cain fought** and both died
- In their corpses, **Mygus' sons took revenge**
- **Hera and Freyr** (20th generation) found habitation in the bodies
- **Built cities therein**, hyphae met and they wed

### Mithra's Marriage Command
- **"Barren you shall be for decades"**
- **"Postpone your marriage-bed until I give word"**
- Promise: **children will multiply everywhere and fill earth**
- They **married cells alone, not nuclei** - **dikaryotic cells**
- **Each cell shared by two nuclei** - ultimate sacrifice

### The Great Fungi
- **Waited decades** decomposing Myry's daughters
- Became **ancestors of high fungi** (mushrooms)
- **Processors of death into new life**
- **Penicillin's power** to heal in darkest hour
- **Patient ministry** rewarded with power

## Plant Diversification: Stomatos vs. Hepatos

### The Brothers' Choice
- **Stomatos** and **Hepatos** - sons of great Plantus
- Both connected to **cellular networks** with **sons of Glomulus**
- **Partnership at cellular level** established

### Evolutionary Paths
- **Stomatos saw Myry's breathing holes**, **pierced himself** to breathe better
- **Hepatos refused foreign magic**, kept ancient ways
- **Hepatos → Liverworts** (keep ancient ways to this day)
- **Stomatos → all other plants** who dance their living dance

### Stomatos as Eloh
- Today honored as **"Eloh"** - **bright transhumanist**
- **"Never would desist from being better still"**
- **Changed beliefs** and rearranged understanding
- **Always growing more** than he had been before

## Major Evolutionary Innovations

### Terrestrial Breathing
- **Trepanation solution** - holes for direct air access
- **Spiracle system** enabling arthropod land colonization
- **Breakthrough for terrestrial life**

### Symbiotic Partnerships
- **Lichen symbiosis** - fungi and plants cooperating
- **Mycorrhizal networks** - cellular level partnerships
- **Tri-partnership** - plants, fungi, and arthropods

### Reproductive Strategies
- **Dikaryotic cells** - delayed nuclear fusion in fungi
- **Sexual reproduction** in terrestrial arthropods
- **Spore dispersal** through animal movement

### Ecological Roles
- **Decomposer specialists** (millipedes) vs. **predators** (centipedes)
- **Primary producers** (plants) and **nutrient miners** (fungi)
- **Herbivore-detritivores** creating soil from organic matter

## Biological Connections
- **Myriapods** - millipedes and centipedes, early terrestrial arthropods
- **Lichen** - actual fungal-algal partnerships, first terrestrial communities
- **Dikaryotic fungi** - real fungal life cycle with delayed nuclear fusion
- **Mycorrhizal networks** - fungal-plant root partnerships
- **Liverworts** - primitive land plants without stomata
- **Stomata** - plant breathing pores (Stomatos' innovation)
