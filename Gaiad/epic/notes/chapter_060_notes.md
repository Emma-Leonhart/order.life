# Chapter 60 Notes: The Carboniferous Dawn

## Post-Extinction Recovery and Terrestrial Colonization

### Romer's Gap and Recovery
- **Devastation's aftermath** - dark age across fertile land
- **Romer's Gap** - period of reduced fossil record after Late Devonian extinction
- **Hope endured** through bitter days when many died
- **Life's persistence** with banners still unfurled

### The Founding of the Carboniferous

#### Rhea and Her Sons
- **Rhea** - daughter of Silvio, "woman blessed"
- **Showed faithful how to rise** above darkness
- **Carbonifer** bore her two sons: **Romulus and Remus**
- **Warriors of light** in heroic form
- **Defined the age** - hence "Carboniferous"

### The Great Fraternal Conflict

#### The Site Dispute
- **Romulus** - chose **Palatine Hill** rising from Mississippi waters
- **Remus** - chose **small Aventine** below the river, hidden valley
- **Height vs. valley** - light vs. shadow preference
- **Vision of national destiny** vs. humble dwelling

#### The Blinding and Exile
- **Romulus in anger gouged Remus' eyes**
- **"If you will not behold heaven's bright face... then you need never see"**
- **Remus condemned** to darkness under Aventine Hill
- **Served his own will** in shadow through sun and rain

### The Two Lineages

#### Remus' Underground Kingdom
- **Children live in darkness underground**
- **Salty water streams** in divine flows
- **Deep aquifers and wide caverns**
- **Ancient mysteries** keep vigil
- **Patience as guide** through years of secrets

#### Romulus' Terrestrial Dynasty
- **Climbed into the day** upon the land
- **Pierced spiracles** for breathing air
- **Six legs strong** to carry across ground
- **Torn apart** but son inherited dream

### Hexapus and the Continuation

#### The Inheritance
- **Hexapus** - son of Romulus, carried father's dream
- **Kingdom in bright, open air** above dark waters
- **Realm where faithful hearts** distinguish dark from light
- **Choose to dwell in day** where hope never fades

#### Hexapus' Sons
- **Vernus** and **Proturus** - never shirk from duty
- **Proturus** became ancestor of **Coneheads**
- **Cone-shaped heads** held high above crowd
- **Honor sacred vows** with wisdom as guide

- **Romer's Gap** - real 15-million-year gap in tetrapod fossil record
- **Carboniferous Period** - age of coal forests and arthropod gigantism
- **Hexapoda** - six-legged arthropods (insects and relatives)
- **Cave adaptation** - loss of vision, enhanced other senses
- **Aquifer ecosystems** - groundwater fauna
- **Terrestrial arthropods** - land colonization
- **Spiracle systems** - arthropod breathing structures
