# Chapter 025 Notes: Cancer and the Birth of Animals
*Aquarius 25 - Chapter 25 of 364*

## Chapter Content Summary  
This chapter presents cancer (Carcinoma) as both a revolutionary figure and a cautionary tale in the evolution of multicellularity. Born from gamma radiation damage, Carcinoma rebels against the hierarchical system of Volvox-Choan's kingdom, leading to cellular chaos. Her revolution forces the loyal Anima and Metazo to flee and establish the Animal Kingdom with proper cellular governance, including tumor suppressor genes and controlled cell division. The narrative explores the tension between individual cellular freedom and multicellular cooperation.
