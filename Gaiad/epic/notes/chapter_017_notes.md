# Chapter 017 Notes: The Hadean Hellscape
*Sagittarius 17 - Chapter 17 of 364*

## Chapter Content Summary
This chapter details the genealogical expansion from Bacta's lineage through her two daughters Hydra and Terra, representing the major bacterial domains. It traces the evolution of early life forms through scientifically accurate taxonomic relationships, covering the development from primitive bacteria to more complex lineages.

## Bacta's Two Daughters
- **Hydra**: Representing gram-negative bacteria and their diverse lineages
- **Terra**: Representing gram-positive bacteria with their specific characteristics
