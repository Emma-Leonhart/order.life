# Chapter 020 Notes: Late Heavy Bombardment
*Sagittarius 20 - Chapter 20 of 364*

## Chapter Content Summary
This chapter continues the eukaryotic evolution story, showing Euka's reflection on his cellular organelles, then tracing the genealogical expansion through five generations to the emergence of the four major eukaryotic kingdoms: Plants, Fungi, Protists, and Animals. It depicts the transition from Snowball Earth to warmer seas and the diversification of complex life.

## Euka's Reflection on His Children
### Catalog of Organelles
- **Reticulus**: "Built the pathways through my living space" - Endoplasmic Reticulum
- **Flagella**: "Grants to all our line the gift of motion" - Cellular movement
- **Golgi**: "Master of the sorting trade" - Protein processing and shipping
- **Peroxis**: "Breaks the fats with might" - Peroxisome metabolism
- **Vaultess**: "Stores our treasures safe and sound" - Vault organelles
- **Centrioles**: "Dance in perfect sight" - Cell division organization
- **Nucleus**: "Keeper of the scrolls divine" - DNA storage and transcription
