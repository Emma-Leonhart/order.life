# Chapter 015 Notes: Jupiter's Great Migration
*Sagittarius 15 - Chapter 15 of 364*

## Chapter Content Summary
This chapter depicts the transition from the Moon to Earth, introducing the Moon Goddess Usagi/Luna who makes a pact with the daughters of Bios. She promises to prepare an elixir of immortality while they establish civilization on Earth, specifically in the ancient supercontinent Vaalbara and the Jack Hills region.

## Divine Covenant
- **Moon Goddess**: Usagi/Luna offering divine pact
- **Immortality Promise**: "elixir of life by my own hand" to "grant you all immortal lives"
- **Conditional Agreement**: Daughters must "go forth and multiply on Terra's land"
- **Future Reunion**: When ready, they will return to drink Luna's potion
- **Cosmic Purpose**: Connected to ultimate Aster-Andromeda reunion
